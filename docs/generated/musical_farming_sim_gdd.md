## üéÆ Core Gameplay  
### Vision & Pillars  
- **Vision Statement**: Create your own symphony of life in a cozy farming world where music blooms.  
- **Design Pillars**:  
  1. **Creativity**: Empower players to design unique musical symphonies through farming.  
  2. **Relaxation**: Deliver a soothing, low-stress experience with cozy visuals and gameplay.  
  3. **Engagement**: Combine rhythm mechanics and progression to sustain player interest.  
  4. **Social Connection**: Encourage sharing creations through seasonal concerts and NPC interactions.  

### Core Loop  
- **30-Second Loop**: Perform rhythmic farming actions (watering, planting, harvesting) to grow musical plants.  
- **5-Minute Loop**: Manage your farm, grow plants, and hear your symphony evolve.  
- **1-Hour Loop**: Unlock new seeds, experiment with hybrids, and plan for seasonal concerts.  
- **10-Hour Loop**: Showcase your symphony in seasonal concerts, progress through farming cycles, and expand your repertoire.
